Frequently Asked Questions
FAQ

How do I know if therapy is right for me?

Answer: Therapy can be beneficial for anyone experiencing emotional or psychological difficulties. If you feel you are dealing with issues that affect your quality of life, therapy could be helpful.

What is psychological therapy, and how can it help me?

Answer: Psychological therapy is a treatment process that can help you address emotional, behavioral, or relationship issues. A psychologist provides support, tools, and strategies to overcome challenges and improve your mental well-being.

What types of therapy do you offer?

Answer: I offer various therapeutic approaches, including cognitive-behavioral therapy, couples therapy, family therapy, and play therapy (for children). The approach is tailored to your individual needs.

How many therapy sessions will I need?

Answer: The duration of treatment varies from person to person and their needs. Some sessions are short-term, while others may require more time. We will assess your progress together and adjust the plan as needed.

Do you accept health insurance?

Answer: Yes, I accept some health insurance plans. You can check if your insurance covers my services by contacting your insurance company or by reaching out to me directly.

How does a therapy session work?

Answer: Therapy sessions typically last 50 minutes and are conducted confidentially. During the session, we will discuss your concerns and work together to explore solutions and strategies.

What confidentiality measures are applied in therapy sessions?

Answer: All therapy sessions are confidential. As a psychologist, I am legally obligated to maintain the privacy of your sessions, except in exceptional cases that require mandatory reporting.

Do you offer online therapy?

Answer: Yes, I offer online therapy for the convenience of patients who cannot attend in-person sessions. Online therapy remains confidential and effective.

How can I schedule an appointment?

Answer: You can schedule an appointment by calling the phone number provided on this website or by using the contact form on the "Contact" page.
